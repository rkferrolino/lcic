Get in touch with LCIC for campus access

Find your way to LCIC — a dynamic learning hub located at the heart of Cebu. Whether you’re visiting for enrollment, collaboration, or campus tours, our facilities are accessible by various modes of transportation.

How to Get Here

Here’s a clear driving guide from Cebu City to Lapulapu-Cebu International College in Lapu-Lapu (Mactan Island):

🚌

By Public Transportation (From Cebu City)

  • Ride a jeepney or bus bound for Mactan / Lapu-Lapu / Airport
  • Cross via Marcelo Fernan Bridge or Mactan-Mandaue Bridge
  • Get off at Basak / Bankal (LCIC area)
  • Take a tricycle, e-bike or habal-habal to Lapulapu-Cebu International College (Cagodoy Road)
🚗

By Car (From Cebu City)

  • Drive toward Mactan via Marcelo Fernan Bridge or Mactan-Mandaue Bridge
  • Continue along ML Quezon National Highway
  • Turn into Cagodoy Road (Bankal area)
  • Drive straight to Lapulapu-Cebu International College (Ticgahon area)
✈️

From the Airport

From Mactan-Cebu International Airport, take a taxi or Grab to Lapulapu-Cebu International College. Travel time is around 30 minutes, depending on traffic conditions.
