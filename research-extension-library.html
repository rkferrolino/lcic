Research, Extension And Library

The Research, Extension, and Library offices of LapuLapu-Cebu International College serve as pillars of academic excellence, innovation, and community engagement. Together, they support knowledge creation, ethical research, and lifelong learning while translating academic expertise into meaningful societal impact.

Through dynamic research initiatives, responsive community extension programs, and a modern learning library, LCIC empowers students, faculty, and partners to contribute to sustainable development, global collaboration, and transformative education.

Research Office

The Research Office is a central hub supporting LCIC’s research ecosystem. It provides resources, guidance, and infrastructure for faculty, students, and partners to pursue impactful research, foster innovation, and enhance academic excellence.

Vision

To be a dynamic and globally recognized center of research excellence that generates innovative knowledge, fosters interdisciplinary collaboration, and contributes to sustainable local, national, and global development.

Mission

To cultivate a vibrant research culture within the college by empowering faculty, staff, and students to pursue high-quality, ethical, and impactful research that advances knowledge and drives innovation for societal transformation.

Core Values

Value Description
Integrity We uphold honesty, transparency, and ethical responsibility in all research activities.
Excellence We strive for the highest standards in research design, execution, and dissemination.
Innovation We encourage creativity, critical thinking, and the pursuit of new knowledge and technologies.
Collaboration We value partnerships and teamwork across disciplines, sectors, and borders.
Accountability We commit to responsible stewardship of resources and adherence to research standards.
Service We aim to generate research that addresses real-world challenges.
Sustainability We promote environmentally responsible and socially relevant research practices.

Goals

  1. Strengthen institutional research capacity.
  2. Promote interdisciplinary and collaborative research.
  3. Enhance research productivity and visibility.
  4. Ensure research integrity and ethical standards.
  5. Mobilize resources and strengthen research sustainability.
  6. Translate research into innovation and impact.

Objectives

  1. Formulate and implement an institutional research agenda.
  2. Provide technical assistance in proposal development and ethics review.
  3. Establish partnerships with funding agencies and research networks.
  4. Organize research training and mentoring programs.
  5. Develop a research management information system and repository.
  6. Support participation in conferences and publications.
  7. Monitor and evaluate funded research projects.
  8. Integrate research with teaching and community engagement.

About the LCIC Library

The LCIC Library serves as a dynamic learning hub that supports academic excellence and lifelong learning. It houses more than 6,000 books and a wide range of collections, including reference books, Filipiniana, general references, fiction, journals, and magazines that cater to the diverse needs of students, faculty, and researchers.

The library takes pride in its specialized collection of Japanese and Korean language resources, strengthening LCIC’s commitment to global education and cross-cultural understanding. With its rich and carefully curated holdings, the LCIC Library provides a conducive environment for study, research, and intellectual growth.

Library Services

LCIC Library offers a wide range and innovative services that cater to both local and international community needs. These services go beyond just lending books and include various educational, informational, and recreational promoting lifelong learning, and providing a welcoming space for all.

Comprehensive Description of Different Library Services

Access to Electronic Resources

LCIC Library supports the instructional and research programs of the Lapulapu-Cebu International College. The Library collects or provides access to materials in multiple formats, including electronic formats. This service is design to monitor the access of the e-resources subscribed by LCIC.

Subscriptions to academic journals, newspapers, e-books, audiobooks, and other digital resources that patrons can access both within the library and remotely.

Circulation

A vital component of library which makes it simple for library users to check out and return and renew items from the collection. The Librarians are responsible for this function, assisted by student library assistants.

  • Returning
  • Renew

Reference & Information Service

The cornerstone of a library's offerings are its reference services, which provide clients looking for information crucial support and direction.

  • One on One / Group Discussion
  • Bibliographic Assistance
  • Virtual Reference Assistance

Information Dissemination

This service guarantees that library patrons have access to pertinent, timely, and reliable information.

  • New Acquisitions

  • New Books Display

Information Literacy

Library information literacy programs aim to provide patrons the abilities and know-how needed to locate, assess, utilize, and organize information.

  • Library Orientation
  • Library Instruction

This service is designed to make it convenient for visitors to charge their gadgets while utilizing the library's resources.
