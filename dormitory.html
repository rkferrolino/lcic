Student Life Dormitory

Welcome to LCIC Dormitory

“A Home Where Comfort Meets Care and Excellence”

At the heart of Lapulapu-Cebu International College (LCIC) stands a space that’s more than just a place to sleep—it’s a thoughtfully designed living environment that nurtures, supports, and empowers its residents to grow and thrive. The LCIC Dormitory Department takes pride in offering a home where world-class facilities, dedicated services, and a strong sense of community come together to create an unmatched student experience.

The experience begins at our Dormitory Assistance Counter, a warm and welcoming space that reflects the care and professionalism we uphold every day. More than just a reception desk, this is the central hub of support and coordination—a place where students are heard, guided, and cared for. Whether it's for daily concerns, urgent matters, or simply needing someone to talk to, the assistance counter serves as a vital lifeline available round the clock. Adjacent to it are the office and private room of our house parents, further strengthening the immediate availability of help, supervision, and emotional support when needed.

Dormitory Services & Management

A Home That Supports Your Dreams.

Our dormitory is more than just a place to stay—it’s a caring and well-managed home. The Dormitory Head Office ensures smooth day-to-day operations, while our House Parents are available 24/7 to offer support and guidance. With genuine care and compassion, our team treats every resident like family, creating a safe, warm, and nurturing environment.

From the Dormitory Head

I used to think my role was purely operational—ensuring rooms were clean, services were running, and systems were in place. But I’ve come to realize that it’s so much more. I am here to mentor, to listen, to build trust, and to remind each student that they have value and purpose. I lead not just with protocols—but with heart.

Maria Concepcion Paguidopon

Dormitory Head

Spread across five floors, the dormitory houses 82 clusters, each thoughtfully designed with six private rooms and one shared room that accommodates up to four students. This setup allows for both privacy and community, catering to the needs of local and international students alike. The cluster design ensures a sense of intimacy and security while still being part of a vibrant, diverse student community.

Our high-caliber facilities are among the finest in the region. Each floor is equipped with common toilets, private showers, and designated wash-and-dry areas. Situated at the top floor area, you will find the male and female pool baths and relaxing saunas that offer a unique wellness feature not commonly found in academic accommodations, underlining our commitment to physical and mental well-being.

The Cluster Units

A Cozy Haven Awaits!

The dormitory features 82 residential clusters, each designed to accommodate 10 occupants with a thoughtful mix of privacy and community. Each cluster includes six private rooms for solo residency and one shared room for four occupants, all fully furnished with individual air-conditioning units, tables, chairs, and cabinets to ensure comfort and functionality. At the heart of each cluster is a cozy common living area equipped with a kitchen countertop, sink, refrigerator, and microwave—an ideal space for residents to prepare snacks, socialize, and enjoy a home-like atmosphere.

Hot and Cold Pools

Dive into Luxury!

Enjoy a refreshing swim in our temperature-controlled indoor and outdoor pools. Perfect for relaxation and exercise.

Sauna Bath

Rejuvenate your Senses!

Experience the ultimate relaxation in our state-of-the-art sauna bath. Ideal for unwinding after a long day.

Laundry Areas

Convenient Laundry Facilities!

Ample and well-equipped laundry facilities are available, featuring Self-service Wash and Dry Stations for residents’ convenience. The area also includes easy access to washing machines, as well as a flat iron and ironing board for added comfort and practicality.

Showers and Smart Toilets

Modern Comfort!

Enjoy our private and individual showers and smart toilets for your ultimate retreat.

Academic success begins with the right environment, and our dedicated study hall provides students with a quiet, focused space to pursue their goals. Meanwhile, the dining hall encourages not only nourishment but also camaraderie among residents—because shared meals often lead to shared stories and friendships.

Dining and Study Hall

Where Minds Grow and Connections Flourish!

Nestled side by side with intention and care, our Dining and Study Halls create a seamless blend of comfort, convenience, and purposeful design. The Dining Hall welcomes students into a bright, spacious setting where shared meals become moments of connection and community. Just a few steps away, the Study Hall offers a serene, well-lit haven—perfectly suited for focused learning and academic excellence. Together, these thoughtfully curated spaces nurture both body and mind, inspiring healthy habits, personal discipline, and a vibrant sense of camaraderie.

A standout feature is our in-dorm clinic, located within one of the clusters. Manned by a professional nurse during night shifts and supported by school nurses during the day, it ensures that medical needs are addressed promptly and compassionately, especially for international students far from home.

24-Hour Clinic

Health and Wellness!

On-site clinic offering 24-hour assistance with professional medical staff ready for emergencies and routine care.

Every service we provide – be it a fresh room, a warm meal, a health checkup, or even a listening ear- reflects our commitment to making the LCIC Dormitory more than a residence. It is a sanctuary where students can rest, recharge, and reconnect with themselves and others.

But beyond facilities, it’s the people and services that define the LCIC Dormitory. Our house parents are available 24/7, offering a reassuring presence and a sense of home for students living away from their families. Our security team ensures a safe and protected environment, while our utility personnel keep the entire dorm clean, organized, and always welcoming.

What truly sets our dormitory apart, however, is the deep commitment to mentorship and guidance that goes far beyond logistics. While we are tasked with overseeing day-to-day operations, we are equally called to respond to the emotional, mental, and behavioral needs of each resident with compassion and attentiveness.

Security & Access System

Secure Spaces for Bright Futures.

The safety and peace of mind of our residents come first. Our dormitory is protected 24/7 by dedicated security personnel and comprehensive CCTV monitoring on every floor. Entry and exit are controlled through a secure ID access system, ensuring only authorized individuals can enter. Together, advanced technology and vigilant staff create a safe, private living environment where residents can feel at ease.

At LCIC, we recognize that many students come carrying unseen burdens – personal challenges, emotional vulnerabilities, or simply the weight of adjusting to a new environment. In these moments, a simple gesture of belonging, a listening ear, or a quiet presence becomes a powerful form of support. We treat each resident not just as students, but as young individuals entrusted to our care – like our very own children. Guided by years of wisdom, life experience, and an unwavering belief in every student’s purpose, we meet each situation with sensitivity, patience, and affirmation. We believe that discipline is most effective when rooted in empathy, and that every moment of correction can also be a moment of connection. Our approach empowers students not only to adjust to communal life but also to grow in character, self-awareness, and confidence.

It is in this environment – where comfort meets care, and structure meets soul – that students find more than shelter. They find affirmation. They find motivation. They find themselves.

As we relaunch our official LCIC website, we invite you to discover this dynamic part of student life through a refreshed lens. With captivating visuals, testimonials from residents, and a virtual glimpse into daily life at the dormitory, the LCIC Dormitory Department page will stand as a proud symbol of the school’s commitment to excellence in student support. Because here at LCIC, we don’t just offer a place to stay – we provide a place to belong.
