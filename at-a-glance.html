What is Lapulapu-Cebu International College (LCIC)?

Lapulapu-Cebu International College (LCIC) is an international institution committed to providing globally relevant education in a diverse and multicultural environment. More than just a vocational or language school, LCIC offers a unique academic experience where Filipino and international students come together to study English while pursuing specialized fields of study. Through an English-integrated curriculum, students develop strong communication skills alongside their academic and professional competencies. LCIC promotes cross-cultural understanding, global awareness, and real-world readiness by encouraging students to live and learn together in a dynamic international community. With a focus on academic excellence, personal growth, and global engagement, LCIC prepares students to succeed in an increasingly interconnected world.

Historical Background of
Lapulapu-Cebu International College (LCIC)

Lapulapu-Cebu International College (LCIC) was born from a forward-thinking vision rooted in a deep commitment to education and global competitiveness, meant to lay the foundation for a transformative institution in the heart of the Philippines. As a product of a Filipino-Japanese joint venture, LCIC was established to equip students with the skills, knowledge, and multilingual proficiency necessary to thrive in an increasingly interconnected world.

Situated on the vibrant island of Mactan, a renowned hub for tourism and language learning, LCIC was designed to bridge local talent with global opportunities. The college aspires to mold students into highly skilled professionals capable of making valuable contributions to society.

History Timeline

July 2026

Graduation of our first batch of graduates from BSPT, BSTM and BAFL

August 12, 2025

Ground Breaking Ceremony of the LCIC Civil Engineering Building hosted by Aboitiz Construction, Inc. (ACI)

March 2025

Government Permit to operate is issued for the Bachelor of Arts in English Language Studies for Academic Year 2025 to 2026

July 2024

Accreditation from Bureau of Immigration authorizing to admit foreign students to the Baccalaureate Programs

July 2024

Government Permit authorizing LCIC to operate Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering is issued for Academic Year 2024 to 2025.

February 2024

Government Recognition is granted LCIC for Bachelor of Science in Physical Therapy, Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management and Bachelor of Arts in Foreign Language.

May 2022

Special Study Permit accreditation from BOI is granted

October 2021

CHED Temporary Permit to Operate was issued for Bachelor of Science in Physical Therapy, Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management and Bachelor of Arts in Foreign Language.

September 2021

LCIC officially opened its doors to students, with the first and second floors of the Foreign Languages Building completed. Additional student halls and other facilities were expected to be finished by the end of the said year.

November 10, 2020

A framework completion and ground-purifying ceremony was conducted, symbolizing the institution’s solid foundation. This event was attended by LCIC stakeholders and contractors, celebrating the progress of three major buildings: student halls (820-person capacity), the faculty building (6,130㎡), and the main building (3,020㎡).

December 1, 2019

The groundbreaking ceremony for LCIC was held in Mactan Island, marking the beginning of its journey to becoming a premier institution.

Why LCIC?

A World-Class Ecosystem

Experience an education that transcends borders. With a diverse international student body and a curriculum rooted in cutting-edge research, we provide an immersive environment where local talent is polished for the global stage.

Precision Programs for Modern Careers

From the intricate allied medical science program to high-stake Engineering degrees, Tourism Management, and Foreign Languages. All programs are laser-focused on industry demand. We don’t just teach; we prepare you to lead and succeed.

The Strategic Edge

Nestled in the heart of the Philippines’ premier tourism and business gateway, LCIC offers unparalleled access to elite professional networks, high-value internships, and real-world career pipelines.

State-of-the-Art Living and Learning

Flourish on a sprawling 6.5-hectare digital-ready campus. From high-tech laboratories, purposefully designed learning spaces, and premium on-campus dormitories, every square inch of LCIC is built for your success.
