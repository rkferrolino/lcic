The Gateway to Your Global
Learning Experience

Welcome to Lapulapu-Cebu International College (LCIC), a premier academic destination where the vibrant spirit of Mactan converges with world-class standards of excellence. Guided by a distinctive Japanese–Filipino educational synergy and supported by a distinguished circle of globally respected educators and industry leaders, LCIC stands at the crossroads of tradition, innovation, and global relevance. Situated along the historic and dynamic shores of Mactan in Lapu-Lapu City, Cebu, LCIC is more than a college—it is a multicultural ecosystem and a high-energy learning hub dedicated to shaping the next generation of leaders, innovators, and global citizens.

Your journey doesn't just start here—it accelerates here. At LCIC, we are bridging communities, shattering glass ceilings, and empowering a new generation of multilingual, industry-ready professionals.

Message from the President

Welcome to Lapulapu-Cebu International College.

At Lapulapu-Cebu International College, we believe that education must go beyond graduation—it must empower individuals to become globally competent, professionally prepared, and culturally grounded citizens of the world. Our institution was established with a clear vision: to deliver quality education anchored in foreign language proficiency, international exposure, and industry relevance.

We take pride in offering programs that are responsive to the evolving demands of the global workforce, while fostering a learning environment that upholds discipline, integrity, and excellence. Our commitment is to open pathways for our students—both locally and internationally—by equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to thrive in an increasingly interconnected and dynamic world.

As President, I am dedicated to strengthening partnerships across borders, enhancing the quality of our academic programs, and ensuring that every LCIC graduate receives an education that is practical, relevant, and transformative.

We are not merely building an institution—we are shaping futures, expanding opportunities, and nurturing globally minded leaders.

Prof. Ethel Agnes P. Valenzuela, EdD, CESO IV

President of Lapulapu-Cebu International College
