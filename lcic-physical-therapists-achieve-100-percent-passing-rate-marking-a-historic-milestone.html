LCIC Physical Therapists Achieve 100 Percent Passing Rate, Marking a Historic Milestone

Lapulapu-Cebu International College (LCIC), proudly celebrates a historic milestone as its first batch of Physical Therapy graduates achieved a 100 percent passing rate in the December 2025 Physical Therapists Licensure Examination. This exceptional accomplishment highlights the institution’s commitment to academic excellence and reflects the quality of its Physical Therapy program.

The graduates’ remarkable success was formally recognized during the Opening of Classes and Convocation Program for the Second Semester of Academic Year 2025-2026, held on January 12, 2026, at Tsuneishi Hall. The event brought together students from various academic programs, faculty members, program chairs, and support staff, who gathered as one LCIC community to celebrate excellence and welcome the new academic term. The seven newly licensed Physical Therapists recognized during the program were:

  • Frances James Monteroso, PTRP
  • Krystine Arante, PTRP
  • June Therese Maputol, PTRP
  • Danica Kaye Demape, PTRP
  • Micah Martinez, PTRP
  • Chiles Jianne Dabon, PTRP
  • Alyssa Gaye Paninsoro, PTRP

In recognition of their outstanding achievement, each board passer received a plaque of recognition and a cash incentive of Php 50000. The awards were presented by Capt. Joven Vanguardia, Ad Interim President; Dr. Angelo C. Maduli, Executive Vice President; Dr. Sharon S. Monsale, School Registrar; Ms. Ivy Marie Abao, Academic Manager; and Ms. Odette Justo, BSPT Program Chair.

The program was further enriched by an inspirational message delivered by Frances James Monteroso, PTRP, who expressed heartfelt gratitude and shared reflections on faith, perseverance, courage, and trust in God’s perfect timing. Her message reminded the LCIC community that meaningful success is built not only on academic achievement but also on character, values, and purpose.

The celebration of excellence continued on January 17, 2026, at 1:00 PM, when the Physical Therapy board passers took their Oath of Adherence at the Philippine International Convention Center (PICC). The oath-taking ceremony marked a significant transition as the graduates formally entered the profession as licensed Physical Therapists, committing themselves to uphold the ethical standards, responsibilities, and integrity of their field.

The momentous occasion was witnessed by Dr. Grace Gorospe Jamon, LCIC President; Ms. Jennica Louise Salazar, Manila based Internship Coordinator; and Maam Odette Justo, PT Program Chairperson, who expressed pride and confidence in the graduates as they reached this important professional milestone.

As newly licensed healthcare professionals, the LCIC Physical Therapists are now well prepared to contribute meaningfully to the healthcare sector by applying their knowledge, clinical expertise, and compassion in service to their patients and the communities they will serve.

As LCIC continues the Second Semester of Academic Year 2025-2026 and welcomes new students, faculty, and staff, this historic achievement stands as a testament to the institution’s mission of nurturing excellence, integrity, and service. The success of the Physical Therapy graduates serves as an inspiration to the entire LCIC community to move forward with unity, dedication, and shared purpose.

Congratulations to the new Physical Therapists of Lapulapu-Cebu International College. May this milestone mark the beginning of impactful careers dedicated to healing, service, and lifelong learning.
