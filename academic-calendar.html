Academic Calendar

The LCIC School Calendar outlines important academic dates and milestones for the Academic Year 2025–2026, as officially approved by the School President. It serves as a guide for students and faculty to plan their studies and activities throughout the academic year. The calendar includes key schedules such as enrollment periods, start and end of classes, examinations, academic breaks, holidays, and institutional events. By following the official calendar, the LCIC community ensures smooth academic operations, timely completion of requirements, and effective coordination of school-wide activities.

MORPHE Article XVII Section 78 School Calendar: The academic year for collegiate courses shall consist of not less than 36 weeks, or 18 weeks a semester, or its equivalent of normally 6 school days each week, exclusive of approved vacations and inclusive of legal and special holidays and days for special activities. In any case, the total number of days on which regular school sessions are held shall not be less than 200 or 100 each semester, exclusive of all holidays and registration days, and class days suspended due to natural or man-made causes.
